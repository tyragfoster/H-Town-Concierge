About Us

  H-Town Concierge is the collaborative work of four University of Houston DIGM 2351 Web Design students, who sought to provide a service that reaches a broad audience and fosters a sense of community. Our user-friendly platform serves as a concierge, to help users learn about Houston's cultural diversity and dynamicity, by showcasing things to do in and around the city. Additionally, this website is a user-friendly tool that allows promoters to recommend activities that users can tailor to their interests and locations.
